## 2. Notwithstanding section 10(1) of the Law, an eGambling licensee[,

Category 1 associate certificate holder] and/or a [Category 2 associate certificate]
holder complying with any conditions attached to its eGambling licence or certificate
and acting in accordance with the provisions of this Ordinance and of regulations
made under this Ordinance is thereby authorised to conduct a business in the course

stranger and to permit a stranger to participate in any form of gambling lawfully
conducted, organised or promoted by that licensee.
